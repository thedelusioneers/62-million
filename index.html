The vocabulary places the harm somewhere peripheral — somewhere you would have to go looking for it. The vocabulary is wrong. And the wrongness matters.

The record

This is the place the vocabulary calls a dark corner.

It is a site in the same traffic tier as a national broadcaster. The scale at which it operates is the scale at which the harm is hosted. The two are not separable.

The 62 million figure comes from CNN's March 2026 As Equals investigation. It represents total monthly visits to the site in February 2026, as reported by CNN's analysis. This document places that number in context. It draws no conclusions about individuals. It documents scale.

Methodology

All traffic figures are drawn from public third-party web analytics tools (primarily Similarweb and Semrush) and industry rankings of the most-visited sites as of early 2026. Numbers are rounded into ranges (e.g., "~60M," "500–700M") to reflect that these tools estimate total visits rather than report exact first-party data. The 62 million monthly-visits figure comes from CNN's March 2026 As Equals investigation, which cites February 2026 traffic to the site in question.
